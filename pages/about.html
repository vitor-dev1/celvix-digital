Our story

We only eat
when you close.

Celvix Digital was founded on a simple frustration: real estate agents were paying marketing agencies thousands a month and getting likes instead of listings. The incentives were broken. Agencies got paid to be busy, not to sell.

So we rebuilt the model. We specialize exclusively in real estate, and we partner with agents on a commission basis — 0.5% of each property closed through our strategies, negotiated individually. We don't profit from retainers. We profit from your closings. That single decision changes everything about how we work.

What we believe

Values that keep us honest.

Aligned incentives

Commission-based means we only get paid when you close. No retainers, no padding hours — just shared upside. We eat when you eat.

Sales over vanity

We don't chase likes. Every campaign, post, and call is measured against one number that matters: properties closed under your name.

Selective partnership

We qualify every agent before we accept them. If we can't genuinely move your numbers, we'll tell you. Fewer partners, better results.
